Learn

New or original vehicle loan amount:

If you want to see what the weekly payment would be on a new vehicle loan, enter the new car loan amount. If you have an existing vehicle loan and want to see how much you could save by switching to a bi-weekly or weekly payment, enter the original vehicle loan amount. Enter as a dollar amount without the dollar sign and commas.

 Annual interest rate:

Enter the annual percentage rate (APR) of the car loan. Enter as a percentage without the percent sign (for 6%, enter 6).

 Yehicle loan term (in years):

Enter the term of the new or original car loan in number of years.

 Number of monthly payments already made:

If this is an existing vehicle loan, enter the number of monthly payments you have already made. If this is a new vehicle loan, enter a zero or leave blank.

 Extra amount you could add to weekly payment (optional):

If you would like to see how adding an extra weekly amount would affect the payoff time and interest savings, enter the amount here. Note that it may help to calculate the weekly payment amount and savings without an extra payment first, and then if it appears you can afford an extra weekly amount, come back to this field enter the amount you can afford and recalculate.
